保研夏令营/预推免英语面试常见问题整理

保研夏令营/预推免英语面试常见问题整理

本博客整理了笔者在2025年6-9月参与高校夏令营期间整理的英语面试问题。

可根据自身情况做对应的修改。

笔者认为在英语面试过程中主要考察的是基本口语，因此建议不要使用复杂句式，避免影响流畅度。

1 自我介绍

Please introduce yourself.

Good morning/afternoon, dear professors. It is my honor to be here for this interview.

My name is {your name}，going to graduate from {school name}. I am majoring in Software Engineering now. During my past 3-year study in college, I have not only built a solid foundation of computer science but also particularly interested on deep learning, program analysis and verification. I ever had a chance to participate in writing of a paper, which is about program termination analysis. I think it’s an interesting but challenging job. I really appreciate this experience since I have learned a lot from it.

In my opinion, I’m a optimistic and determined person. So If I am lucky enough to be admitted today, I will study hard in and I am willing to achieve a higher goal in next few years. That’s all for my self-introduction. Thanks for your attention.

2 未来规划

Do you know our school? / Why do you choose to study at our school ？

First, here are lots of great teachers and study environment to support me in my future research.

Second, I’m interested in this laboratory’s research focus, especially on program analysis and verification.

So, I believe I can do some valuable job here in the future.

Why do you want to become a postgraduate ?

Because of my personality.

I have ever visited some company in {city}. I find that I’m not fond of the competitive atmosphere in company.

However, I prefer focusing on research and studying.

I hope I can do some valuable researches in the future, rather than just simple software development.

So I decide to give myself more years to study and enhance my ability.

What is your research plan ? / What do you expect to achieve if you are enrolled into this institute ?

First, I will read a lot of high-level papers and conduct experiments under totur’s guidance.

Second, I will participate in writing academic papers.

In addition, if possible I would like to have some opportunities for international academic communication.

Do you have a career plan ?

After graduation, I will probably find a related job in a company in {city} or apply for a PHD.

Actually, I don’t have a clear plan now, but I will make a decision in my postgraduate stage.

I am very particularly interested in applying the theoretical knowledge to practice.

For example, it would be fun to use software verification tools to check the safety of a railway system.

How many programs did you apply for ? If we give offer to you, will you accept ?

Of Course, if I get offer, I will definitely come.

I only contact a teacher of my current university before.

Actually it’s my first summer camp today.

The rich study resource here is very attractive to me. So if I am lucky enough, I will come.

You can ask any questions that you might have ?

Can you tell me about some work on program analysis in recent years? Just one specific example?

3 生活日常

Tell something about your hometown.

My hometown is {city}, located in the northeast part of {province}.

In summer, it’s actually pretty hot and humid there. But compared to {city}, winters in my hometown are much warmer.

I love my hometown because it’s a coastal city. And the beautiful ocean always make me relax.

Tell something about your family.

There are three people in my family: my dad, my mom, and me.

My dad is really smart, and my mom is very thoughtful and kind.

They’re now running a building materials business in {province}.

My parents always respect my ideas and never forced me to do things I don’t like.

I feel lucky to have such a family, and my parents love me so much.

Which city do you like best and why ?

My favorite city is Beijing, which is a international city and also decades old.

People from all over the world live there, and everyone gets along peacefully.

And, Beijing also has a strong economy.

There are more opportunities to build a career.

What do you like to do in your spare time ?

In my spare time, I enjoy long-distance running.

No matter any time I run on the playground, I always feel very relaxed.

What’s more, I also grow some green plants.

For instance, in last month, I planted a morning glory on the balcony of the dormitory.

What are your strengths and weaknesses ?

In my opinion, my strength is that I am an optimistic and determined person.

I think I can fully focus on research. And I am willing to achieve a higher goal and not afraid of difficulties.

But I think it’s not an easy thing for me to make a balance between entertainment and work.

Sometimes, one of them takes my most time.

What’s your specialty? / What are good at ?

I believe that I am good at coding and lab work.

I have ever participate in the experiments and writing of a paper.

Given a raw idea, it’s a challenging experience to convert it into actual code and experiments.

In this process, I meet lots of BUGs and difficulties, but I finally figured them out one by one.。

4 项目问题

Tell me something about your major.

My major is Software engineering.

Software engineering is the systematic application of engineering principles to design, develop and test software.

It focuses on building high-quality software efficiently while considering cost and future updates.

Who is your undergraduate tutor ?

My undergraduate tutor is Prof. {name}.

He/She is very kind and has offered a great help to my scientific research.

I am very grateful to him/her.

What’s the biggest challenge / problem in this project ?

The most difficult part of this work is how to {sth.}, so that it {sth.}.

But we finally find a solution to {sth.}.

What are the shortcomings of this project research ?

The shortcoming of our approach is that it doesn’t work for {sth.}.

Since our model is built around **{sth.} **, it can’t handle {sth.} .

What do you think of artificial intelligence ?

I think that the rapid development of AI has brought great convenience to our daily lives.

It is a very promising research direction to combine AI with traditional industry.

For example, automatic drive, computer vision for medical detection and so on.

5 英语学习

How is your English level ？

I study English for 3 years in university and have passed CET-4/GET-6 with a decent score.

I think my English ability would help me in not noly academic study but also future career.

But I still need to keep learning to improve my English spoken and writing level.

How do you practice English ?

I improve my English level by simply reading some English paper.

When I meet some words I don’t know, I search their mearnings on web dictionary and write them down.

What’s more, I also practice my spoken English by watch YouTube channels in my spare time.

For example, some vlogs and English movies.

6 套话

这里给出没听懂问题时的套话。

Sorry I cannot hear clearly.

I don’t really understand this one. It’s difficult for me to answer it.

Could you please express in a different/simper way?

Could you please change a question?
